Our Mission: We hope to bring a better understanding of mathematics to both parents and students, allowing them to navigate the world of numbers and theories more easily. For parents, we have made videos illustrating how students may be learning using new Common Core teaching methods. For students, we provide math flashcards and games with the end goal of philosophical math proficiency. By using the tools available on our website, algebraic manipulation of equations and expressions will come easily to students and allow them to excel in future math classes.
Our Inspiration: Math has not always been taught the way it is now. Before money math, philosophical math was the standard of teaching. As world became more capitalistic, our societies shifted to economical mathematics. Now, with the implementation of Common Core standards, Americans are trying to shift back to the way of the Ancient Greeks. More information can be found in this article.
Money Math Goals: calculation and prediction. examples: fact families; word problems; fundmental addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Artisan Math Goals: measurement. examples: fractions; conversions.

Philosophical Math Goals: concepts and ideas. examples: how many ways can the same number be written?
